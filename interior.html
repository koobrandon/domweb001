KTK DOM RAILWAYS SDN. BHD.

KTK DOM Railways Sdn Bhd is a joint venture between KTK Group Co., Ltd and DOM Industries (M) Sdn Bhd, two very established parties related to the railway industry.

KTK Group Co., Ltd, a giant in rolling stock interior manufacturing which has partnerships internationally and supplied across the global market.

DOM Industries (M) Sdn Bhd, with their experience has successfully implemented projects, supplying parts/equipment and maintenance for Keretapi Tanah Melayu (KTMB), Tenaga Nasional Berhad (TNB), Telekom Malaysia Berhad (TM ) and many others prestigious government and private sectors.

With their best interests aligned, both parties has agreed to proceed into a joint venture to further progress in the railway industry.
