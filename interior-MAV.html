Mission

  • Manufacturing and assembling train interior components which are high in quality to ensure the safty of the public.
  • Every parts manufactured are to be throughly checked for quality before delivery to our clients.
  • Securing upcoming railway projects by using the knowledge, skills, and expertise we have gathered.
  • Providing the best service, quality, and maintenance for every contract.

    • Vision

  • To learn, improve, and teach local Malaysian labour the skills and expertise for railway industry
  • Establishing groundworks to penetrate global railway market in the near future.
