CORPORATE OVERVIEW

DOM was incorporated in Malaysia on 10th October 1980 by a group of high competent people with its registered office at NO. C-3a-2, 4Th floor, Block C, Megan Phoenix, Jalan 2/142A, off 6th Mile, Jalan Cheras, 56000 Kuala Lumpur and its head office at NO.25 Jalan ss15/4, 47500 Subang Jaya, Selangor Darul Ehsan.

DOM is a bumiputera company and registered with ministry of finance Malaysia, Contractor Services Bureau(PKK) and other statuary Bodies.

This past decade, DOM has emerged to become of the leading company in implementing local and international contracts for works and procurement and servicing of plant and equipment.

Dom has successfully implemented projects, supplying parts/equipment and maintenance for Keretapi Tanah Melayu (KTMB), Tenaga Nasional Berhad (TNB), Telekom Malaysia Berhad (TM ) and many others prestigious government and private sectors.

Today, DOM is surging ahead in such diverse fields like, Telecommunications and IT, Power and generation, Lighting and Surge Protection and Railway. DOM boast an impressive growth that can be attribute to a companywide commitment to stringent quality, standards, customer service and product development. We hope to satisfy the needs of customers with continuous stream of innovative products and services.
